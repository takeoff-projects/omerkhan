{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead %}} This Handbook was started in July, 2021. Inspired by the Gitlab handbook, it is intended to be a permanent resource for anyone on the Engineering team at Takeoff. {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section >}}

Who is this handbook for?

 

It is meant for current engineers, new hires to the Takeoff Technologies family, and anyone that wants to learn more about Takeoff and our Engineering group.

{{< /blocks/section >}} {{< blocks/section >}}

Why does this Handbook exist?

 

We all need a helping hand now and then. Joining a new organization, team, domain, or even project can require a lot of learning.

Whether its your first day, last, or 10th anniversary, this handbook is designed to be a one-stop location for any guidance to Takeoff you may need. It encompasses everything from Engineering onboarding, to how to conduct oneself in meetings, and many areas in between.
